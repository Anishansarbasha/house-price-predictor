Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How accurate is the prediction?

Our model has a 64% R² score, meaning it explains 64% of the variance in house prices. It's a useful estimate but should be used alongside professional valuations.

Is this service free?

Yes! This is an academic project and is completely free to use for everyone.

Which cities are covered?

We cover 9 major cities in Tamil Nadu: Coimbatore, Erode, Madurai, Salem, Thanjavur, Thoothukudi, Tirunelveli, Trichy, and Vellore.

How long does prediction take?

Predictions are instant! Our optimized ML model returns results in just 2-3 seconds.
